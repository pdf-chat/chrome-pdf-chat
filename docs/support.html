Frequently asked questions

The chat panel isn't appearing on my PDF

Make sure you're signed in via the extension settings (click the puzzle icon in Chrome's toolbar → PDF Chat → Options). If you're signed in and the panel still doesn't appear, try refreshing the page. Some PDFs embedded inside iframes may take a few seconds to be detected.

I get "Upload failed: Invalid or expired token"

Your session has expired. Open the extension settings, sign out, then sign back in with Google. Sessions last up to 1 hour and are automatically refreshed — if you see this error it means the refresh failed.

The extension can't read my PDF

PDF Chat extracts text from PDFs. It cannot read PDFs that are entirely image-based (scanned documents) or encrypted with a password. If your PDF is image-only, consider running it through an OCR tool first.

How many questions can I ask?

You can ask up to 20 questions per hour. This limit resets automatically. You can also upload up to 10 PDFs per hour.

Is my PDF data stored?

No. PDF text is held in server memory only for the duration of your session (up to 2 hours of inactivity) and is never written to a database or disk. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I sign out or delete my account?

To sign out, open the extension settings and click "Sign Out". To delete your account and all associated data, email us at support@pdfchat.app with the subject "Delete my account".
